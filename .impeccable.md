## Design Context

### Users
- Design students (university level)
- Live presentation via projector/screen

### Brand Personality
- Modern, editorial, design-forward
- Substantive academic content with visual sophistication
- Inspired by norgram.co, tempo.xyz, driftime.com

### Aesthetic Direction
- Dark theme (#09090b background, refined warm gold accent)
- Large bold typography, left-aligned content
- Generous whitespace, asymmetric layouts
- Clean cards with subtle borders
- Important content prominently displayed

### Design Principles
- Content can be simplified for presentation format
- Important points must be visually prominent
- Design should feel premium and intentional
- Maintain readability from distance (projector)
